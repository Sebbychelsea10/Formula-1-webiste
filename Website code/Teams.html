2024 Formula 1 Teams

Mercedes-AMG Petronas

Founded in 2010, Mercedes is one of the most successful F1 teams, dominating much of the 2010s with drivers like Lewis Hamilton.

Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing has won multiple championships, led by star drivers like Sebastian Vettel and Max Verstappen.

Scuderia Ferrari

As the most iconic and oldest team in F1, Ferrari has a rich history, with legendary drivers like Michael Schumacher.

McLaren F1 Team

McLaren has a storied history in F1, known for its innovation and drivers such as Ayrton Senna and Lewis Hamilton.

Aston Martin Cognizant

Aston Martin returned to F1 in recent years, with ambitions of becoming a top contender in the modern era.

Alpine F1 Team

Alpine, formerly Renault, has rebranded and is seeking success in the new era of Formula 1 with drivers like Esteban Ocon.

Vcarb

Vcarb serves as Red Bull’s sister team, focusing on developing young talent and racing in the midfield of the grid.

Audi F1 Team

Audi, has been a consistent midfield contender with drivers like Valtteri Bottas and Zhou Guanyu.

Haas F1 Team

Founded in 2016, Haas is the first American F1 team in decades, with drivers like Kevin Magnussen and Nico Hülkenberg.

Williams Racing

One of F1's legendary teams, Williams has a storied past but is working to return to its former glory in the modern era.
